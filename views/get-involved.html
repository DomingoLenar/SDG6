PARTNERSHIP CAMPAIGNS

This initiative encourages businesses worldwide to adopt sustainable and socially responsible policies, aligning their operations with ten principles in the areas of human rights, labor, environment, and anti-corruption.

SWA is a global partnership that works towards achieving universal access to clean water and sanitation by building strong political will, mutual accountability, and collaboration among governments, donors, civil society, and development partners to support the achievement of SDG 6.

Though officially concluded in 2015, the Water for Life Decade (2005-2015) laid the groundwork for continued global efforts on water and sanitation issues. Its legacy efforts to fulfill international commitments on water and sanitation by raising awareness and encouraging actions.
